About BSN:

Our Manifesto

Our aims is to provide a space for bi survivors to find
solidarity and support from other bi survivors.
To begin with meetings will be once a week
for 90 minutes via telegram.
Links will be given out up request
from the the twitter account, or email.
Eventually, monthly meeting of 90 minutes will be organised,
most likely in London.
