Bisexual Survivors Network

About Us

We are a group for bi survivors of domestic and/or sexual violence, facilitated by other survivors. Our
group offers peer-led, anonymous support for bisexual people of all genders through virtual meetups.
We are not crisis support nor can we offer counselling services. We are a mixed gender group.


You. Are. Not. Alone.

Who we are

We are a network of survivors who identify as bisexual. We offer support, soildarity and community to
one another through weekly virtual chats via Telegram. These chats last 90 minutes and are moderated
by bi survivors. Our aim is to let survivors lead the direction of these chats and share as much or as little
as you feel comfortable with.

Our Manifesto

To provide a space for bi survivors to find solidarity and support from other bi survivors. To begin with
meetings will be once a week for 90 minutes via telegram. Links will be given out up request from the
twitter account or email. Eventually monthly meetings of 90 minutes will be organised, most likely in
London.
